!1 Welcome to Codemanship's App!
This is an application that allows players to design bots for a variety of 2 player board games (Tic tac toe, draughts, chess, backgammon etc) and pit them against each other to see who can program the superior game playing bot!

!2 Waferslim examples
* DecisionTables
* TableTables
* ExceptionHandling
* IterableConversion
* QueryTable
* ScriptTable
* MethodNames

!2 Acceptance tests
* TwoPlayerTicTacToe
* FilteringPlayerQueue
